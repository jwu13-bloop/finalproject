The world did not collapse, it fractured.
After a failed spell meant to save everything, reality rewrote its rules. Magic no longer works alone.

Together, you are witches born into this aftermath, a spell-caster trained to work with fragments of a broken world. Cities are unstable, landscapes unfinished, and paths remain sealed unless multiple wills align. Power now responds only to collaboration.

To move forward, you must cast together, rebuild together, and decide together what this world becomes.

This is not a world to conquer.
It is a world waiting to be remade.
For souls to be saved.
