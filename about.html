About this space

I made this website as a collaborative space in mind. A space where users can uncover things together.

The story doesn’t have to make sense. That’s the point.
You enter fractured realms, glitching spaces, and slow, unpredictable paths.
The real focus is on working together. You and others must align, explore, and experiment to see what unfolds. Some doors stay closed, some fragments only respond when multiple wills meet.

It’s less about winning and more about inhabiting a space where collaboration shapes what comes next.

Sometimes you just got to click and drag everywhere.
