What remained of the world became five trinkets.
Each carrying a function the world can no longer perform on its own...
Hover over them to uncover the meanings

1. Crimson Flicker:
Before the fracture, movement was automatic. Things went somewhere else without needing to be convinced.
2. Twin Crescents:
Memory was damaged in the fracture. Not erased—just scattered. Without it, the world pretends every attempt is the first. Mistakes are repeated. Discoveries vanish.
3. Solar Orbit:
Time used to flow. Now it buffers. Without it, events repeat, pause, or arrive out of order. The world forgets whether something already happened.
4. Mirrored Gate:
Balance once happened quietly. Now it must be maintained. Without it, realms tilt, some become unreachable, others collapse inward.
5. Heartflare:
This was the last to form. Without it, words trigger randomly or not at all. Symbols exist, but don’t mean anything. Intent only activates when players choose the same outcome.
